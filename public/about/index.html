I’m an urban engineer, currently doing smartcity R&D at AREP. My work is to help everybody about all thoses new connected things, should they be architect or electrical engineer, and make sure they make sense for an architectural project. Before that, I did research on the relation between energy fluxes and urban morphology at EIVP and CSTB. I also work on various side-topics regarding urban morphology, urban design/planning and urban climate.
